Counter Attack Cards
Chapter 14 · Taking Authority · Prepared Responses
Prepare NOW — before you need it. After every victory, expect a counter attack. This is where most people lose their ground. These cards are your ammunition. Read them NOW so when the attack comes, your response is already loaded.
"After you've done all — STAND. Don't give back what God gave you. The test gives birth to the testimony. The harder the fight, the greater the story you'll tell." — Chuck Zaagsma, Ch. 14
⚔️ Master Counter Attack Declaration
Speak This First — Every Time
"Devil, you are defeated. My God is greater than this situation. I will not agree with your lies. I stand on God's word. I hold my confession. I WILL NOT QUIT."
Ephesians 6:13 / James 4:7
Common Attacks & Your Responses
The Lie
"I guess it didn't work. Maybe God doesn't want you to have this."
Your Response
"God's word does not return void. It accomplishes what He pleases. I believe I receive, and I will not waver. It IS working."
Isaiah 55:11 / Mark 11:24
The Lie
"Maybe God wants you to suffer. This must be His will."
Your Response
"There is NO sickness in heaven. Jesus said 'Thy will be done ON EARTH as it is in heaven.' Suffering is not my inheritance. Healing IS."
Matthew 6:10 / 1 Peter 2:24
The Lie
"You're not good enough. Look at your past. Who do you think you are?"
Your Response
"I am the righteousness of God in Christ Jesus. My past is under the blood. I am a new creation. Old things have passed away."
2 Corinthians 5:17-21
The Lie
"Nothing ever works out for you. This is just your life."
Your Response
"I am more than a conqueror through Jesus. ALL things work together for good to those who love God. This situation is turning for my good."
Romans 8:28 / Romans 8:37
The Lie
"You'll never have enough. Lack is just your reality."
Your Response
"My God supplies ALL my needs according to His riches in glory. The blessing of the Lord makes rich and adds no sorrow. Abundance is my covenant right."
Philippians 4:19 / Proverbs 10:22
The Lie
"That symptom is back. The healing didn't stick."
Your Response
"By the stripes of Jesus I AM healed — past tense, finished. I don't have this. This is not mine and I refuse to accept it. Body, come into alignment with God's word."
Isaiah 53:5 / 1 Peter 2:24
The Lie
"Everyone is against you. No one is in your corner."
Your Response
"If God is for me, who can be against me? The Lord is my helper. I will not fear what man can do to me. I have an army of angels at work."
Romans 8:31 / Psalm 103:20
The Lie
"Just give up. It's not worth it."
Your Response
"After I have done ALL — I STAND. I do not quit. I do not give up. The harder the battle, the greater the victory. My testimony is being written right now."
Ephesians 6:13 / Revelation 12:11
