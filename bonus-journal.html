Week 1 · Days 1–7
🔍 Awareness
This week you're not changing anything yet — you're just watching. Track what comes out of your mouth. You can't fix what you can't see.
Week 2 · Days 8–14
🔄 Replace
You've seen your patterns. Now you replace them. Every death word gets a life word. Every complaint gets a declaration. Start speaking your destination.
Week 3 · Days 15–21
🏗️ Build
Now you're building. Scriptures, declarations, binding and loosing. You're calling the dog. Your angels are getting employed. This is where it gets real.
Week 4 · Days 22–30
🛡️ Defend
After victory comes the counter attack. This week you hold your ground, journal your wins, and prepare your responses. You don't quit at 30 days — you've just built the foundation.
