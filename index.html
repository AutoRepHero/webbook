About the Author

Chuck Zaagsma is an enthusiastic motivator who has triumphed over what seemed insurmountable odds. Chuck rose up out of the ashes of physical, financial, and emotional ruin — knocking on death's door — to a completely restored soul, free from lifelong addictions and completely transforming every area of his life for the better.


Chuck is first and foremost a man of God. A former correctional officer and US Army and Air Force veteran, God has called him to "arm the troops" and teach His word to His people. He is married to his beautiful wife Pam and has four amazing sons: Josh, Xander, Zachary, and Owen. He is a member of Zeal Church in Spokane, WA.
