Why This Exists
"I was broken. Addicted. In constant physical pain — nerve damage, three shoulder surgeries, damaged vertebrae. A friend gave me a recording of healing scriptures. I listened to it over and over and over again. My body responded to God's word. I lost over 125 lbs. The nerve damage healed. I play guitar now with the same hand I couldn't lift above my head. Faith comes by hearing. These are the confessions I built from that foundation. This is your medicine."
— Chuck Zaagsma, Taking Authority
How to use this: Read these out loud daily — like medicine. You wouldn't forget your medicine. Speak them in present tense, with faith. The more you hear your own voice declaring God's word, the more your faith grows. Romans 10:17 — Faith comes by hearing.
