How to Use This Workbook

This workbook goes chapter by chapter through Taking Authority. For each chapter you'll find a summary, the key points, reflection questions to write your answers, and action steps to check off as you do them.

Your answers save automatically in your browser. Take your time. Do the work. This is where the transformation actually happens — not in the reading, but in the doing.

⚡ 30-Day Transformation Challenge

This is where the book becomes a system. Do the work. Get the results.

Week 1

🔍 Awareness
  • Track every word you say about yourself
  • Identify death words and victim language
  • Memorize Proverbs 18:21

Week 2

🔄 Replace

  • Rename the negative things in your life
  • Speak declarations 3x daily

  • Journal your victories
  • Share your testimony with someone
