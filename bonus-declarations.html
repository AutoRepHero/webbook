Daily Declaration Card
One Power Declaration Per Chapter · Taking Authority
Speak these every morning. Put this on your mirror, your phone wallpaper, or your bathroom wall. One declaration per chapter — all 14 weapons loaded. Speak them out loud. Your voice creates. Proverbs 18:21 — Death and life are in the power of the tongue.
14 Daily Declarations
01
Chapter 1 — Utter Defeat, No Hope
My story is not over. God can restore what seems permanently destroyed. This is my comeback chapter.
02
Chapter 2 — Choosing the Words
I am the author of my life. I choose better words today. Better words, better story, better life.
03
Chapter 3 — Get Out of the Pot
I refuse to be pulled back in. I choose life. My life is a GET TO, not a have to. I claim the goodness God has for me right now!
04
Chapter 4 — Victim or Victor
I speak like a king. I am a victor, not a victim. I keep playing until I win. Where the word of a king is, there is power.
05
Chapter 5 — The Big Lie
Death and life are in the power of MY tongue. I choose life. I eat the fruit of words that build, bless, and create.
Proverbs 18:21
06
Chapter 6 — The Power of Words
I am made in God's image. My words create. I call things by the right name — what I WANT them to be, not what they are.
07
Chapter 7 — Self-Fulfilling Prophecy
I shall have what I SAY. I pray the answer, not the problem. I declare the outcome before the battle.
Mark 11:23
08
Chapter 8 — Steering Wheel
My words are steering my life toward victory, abundance, and God's best. I speak my destination every day.
James 3:3-4
09
Chapter 9 — Unemployed Angels
My angels are employed. I speak God's word over every situation. Heaven's army is working on my behalf right now.
Psalm 103:20
10
Chapter 10 — Patterned Behavior
I am rewiring my word patterns. Every day I catch and replace. 30 days builds a new normal. I am building mine now.
11
Chapter 11 — Calling the Dog
I call the thing I WANT, not the thing I have. I have ___________. I speak it in present tense. I receive it now by faith.
Romans 4:17 / Mark 11:24
12
Chapter 12 — Being Specific
I bind ___ from operating in my life. I loose God's blessing, favor, and provision. I am specific. I am terrific.
Matthew 16:19
13
Chapter 13 — Claiming Your Inheritance
I am an heir of God. His promises are mine — HERE and NOW. I live like an heir, not an orphan. THIS IS MINE.
Galatians 3:29 / Romans 8:17
14
Chapter 14 — Counter Attacks
Devil, you are defeated. My God is greater than this situation. I stand. I hold my confession. The test gives birth to the testimony.
Ephesians 6:13
